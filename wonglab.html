Wong Biomedical Imaging Lab

Biomedical imaging and computational research focused on circulating tumor cell morphology, image analysis workflows, and quantitative biological data interpretation.

Research Overview

In the Wong Biomedical Imaging Lab, I worked on research involving circulating tumor cell morphology analysis and computational imaging workflows. My work focused on understanding how extracellular matrix stiffness influences tumor cell behavior and morphology using quantitative image analysis methods.

What I Did

  • Analyzed circulating tumor cell morphology using CellProfiler and Cellpose
  • Worked with fluorescence microscopy imaging workflows
  • Performed quantitative image analysis and PCA analysis
  • Investigated morphology changes on soft versus stiff matrices
  • Assisted with computational biological data interpretation
